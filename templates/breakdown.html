What is CampHub?

CampHub is the first web site to foster an environemnt where bootcamp students (or prospective students) from all fields can come together and chat about all things bootcamp related!

Here, you can seek guidance, chat about frustrations, success stories, ask technical questions, and more!

There are two ways to interact with CampHub. If you would like to create your own discussion topics you're encouraged to create an account here. If you're only looking to interact with the moderator articles and create a WordPress account you may join CampHub Wordpress.

Why was CampHub created?

During my time as a Peer Organizer I had the pleasure of working with many software development students coming from a vast variety of different backgrounds and skill levels. It was then that I heard many of the same sentiments from the students. These include:

  • "I feel alone."
  • "I think the other student's understand and I don't."
  • "I'm struggling with this project and don't know what to do."
  • "I'm afraid to ask questions"/ "I don't know how to phrase my questions."

Then, I thought... Let's fix that. In an effort to create a positive and uplifting environemnt for students to connect CampHub was created. Here, you can find your way through the bootcamp world whether you're simply thinking about jumping into a bootcamp or a few months in and looking for a network of people just like you.
