CampHub is a blog site originally hosted under WordPress. In an effort to create a safe environemnt for students to ask questions or provide honest feedback about a bootcamp we have seperated the users as to allow for 'camphub-users' and 'wordpress-users'. This means users who post here are deemed camphub-users (privately for application users only) and users who post under Wordpress are 'wordpress-users' (public for all WordPress users). Users should use their preferred site to interact with the content.

What are the differences?

Posts

Wordpress does not allow for blog users to read and create posts. Only the blog creator may do this. Wordpress users may only comment on posts.

Camphub-users are provided the option to create their own posts within this application! This allows for more topics and further community interaction. Here, you can post discussion items, questions, or topics you would to receive feedback on. If you would like to discuss a topic but do not feel comfortable posting about it, you may complete the suggest a topic. form and the CampHub moderator will create the post on your behalf. No names/user details will be shared.

Comments

All post comments will be visible here, whether they be made through WordPress or Camphub. However, you may only make comments and respond to comments through the website you are accessing. For example, if you are using CampHub, you may read comments from CampHub and WordPress but may only comment on CampHub comments made here.

If you are on WordPress you may only read and respond to comments made through WordPress. Camphub-user comments are not visible.
