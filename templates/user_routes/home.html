Why was Camphub created?

In an effort to create a safe environemnt for students, or prospective-students to ask questions, provide honest feedback about a bootcamp, create their own topics of discussion, as well as interact with CampHub Wordpress.

What are the differences between Wordpress and Camphub?

Articles and Posts

Wordpress article topics are created by the moderator only. Users are only permitted to make comments under moderator articles.

Here, Camphub-users are provided the option to view and comment on Wordpress article posts, in addition to creating their own posts within this application. This allows for more topics and further community interaction. You're welcome to post discussion items, questions, or topics you would like to receive feedback on. If you would like to discuss a topic but do not feel comfortable posting about it, you may complete the suggest a topic form and the CampHub moderator will create the post on your behalf. No names/user details will be shared.

Comments

Wordpress only allows comments from Wordpress users.

Here, Camphub-users may respond to Wordpress articles. Those comments will only exist here, in Camphub. They are not published to Wordpress, so no wordpress account is required, and you remain within this community with further flexibility than Wordpress.
